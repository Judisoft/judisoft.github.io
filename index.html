Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our WhatsApp bill payment service. If you have any other questions, feel free to contact us through WhatsApp.

1. How do I start using WhatsPay for bill payments?

Getting started is simple! Just save our WhatsApp number and send a "Hi" message. Our automated assistant will guide you through the registration process. You'll need to provide basic information and set up your payment method. Once registered, you can start paying bills instantly through WhatsApp.

2. What types of bills can I pay through WhatsPay?

You can pay a wide range of bills including utility bills (electricity, water, gas), mobile recharges, credit card bills, rent payments, and entertainment subscriptions. We're constantly adding more billers to our network. You can check the complete list of supported billers by sending "Bills" to our WhatsApp number.

3. Is it safe to pay bills through WhatsApp?

Yes, WhatsPay uses bank-grade encryption and security measures to protect your transactions. We never store your payment details on WhatsApp. All transactions are processed through secure payment gateways, and you'll receive instant confirmation for every payment. Additionally, you can enable two-factor authentication for extra security.

4. What payment methods are accepted?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and digital wallets. You can add multiple payment methods to your account and choose your preferred option for each transaction. All payment methods are processed through secure payment gateways to ensure your financial information is protected.

5. What if I need help with a payment or have an issue?

Our customer support team is available 24/7 through WhatsApp. Simply send "Help" to our number, and you'll be connected with a support agent. For urgent issues, you can use the "Priority Support" feature available in our Business and Enterprise plans. We typically respond within minutes and resolve most issues within the same chat.
